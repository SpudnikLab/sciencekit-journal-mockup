Features

Everything for field research

From sensor auto-connect to citizen science publishing — built for the way researchers actually work.

📡

Real-time Sensor Recording

Auto-connect to USB & Bluetooth sensors. Record soil moisture, CO₂, temperature, and audio data with timestamped precision.

📓

Multi-Journal Organization

Create separate journals for each research project. Tag entries by type — sensor, photo, audio, or written observation.

🌍

Citizen Science Publishing

Share findings directly to ISRIC WoSIS, iNaturalist, and SoilGrids with one click. Contribute to global research databases.

🔗

P2P Collaboration

Invite co-researchers, share journal access, and co-author entries in real-time. No server required — peer-to-peer sync.

📊

Data Export & Archive

Export entries to CSV or standard formats. Archive completed journals while keeping them searchable and accessible.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Designed for field use. Works fully on mobile browsers — log observations on-site, sync when back at base.

How it works

From field to findings in three steps

A streamlined workflow designed around how field researchers and citizen scientists actually collect data.

Step 01

Connect & Record

Plug in your ScienceKit sensor or connect via Bluetooth. The journal auto-detects your device and starts live data capture — soil moisture, CO₂, temperature — all timestamped and logged automatically.
